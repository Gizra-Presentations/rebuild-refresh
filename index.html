Aspect #1

It seeks to achieve a business goal.

A business goal is not:

I want to drive more users to my site

 

A business goal is:

I want to capture more signups to my mailing list

or I want to increase the number of available volunteers

Aspect #2

The goals are framed in human-centered terms.
